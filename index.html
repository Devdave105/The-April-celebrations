Our Story

More Than a Restaurant.
A Mood. A Movement.

The April Celebrations — known intimately as The April Signature — was born from an obsession with beauty, refinement, and the belief that every meal deserves to be a memory. Nestled in the prestigious Ewet Housing Estate in Uyo, we've crafted a sanctuary where Plum Noir tones, floral motifs, and candlelit intimacy collide with world-class cuisine.

Our signature restaurant, Eden Bay London, brings globally-inspired flavors to your plate, while our Sunshine Spa ensures you leave lighter than you arrived. This isn't just dining — this is an experience you will wear on your soul for years.
